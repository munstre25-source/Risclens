# RiscLens Flagship Buyer Path

RiscLens is a buyer-intent decision engine for revenue-blocked B2B teams. The flagship path reflects this canonical journey:

## Phase 1: Reality Check (Entry)
**Question Answered:** "Are we actually audit-ready, or guessing?"
**Tool:** SOC 2 Readiness Calculator
**Intent:** Frame as the recommended starting point for serious buyers to establish a baseline of reality.

## Phase 2: Business Impact (Decision Support)
**Question Answered:** "What does this mean commercially?"
**Tools:**
- SOC 2 Cost Calculator
- SOC 2 Timeline Estimator
- Compliance ROI Calculator
**Intent:** Contextualize readiness results into money, time, and ROI for internal justification.

## Phase 3: Execution Clarity (Doing)
**Question Answered:** "What exactly do we need to do next without screwing this up?"
**Tools:**
- Pentest Scoping Worksheet
- Pentest Cost Estimator
- Pentest SOW Template
- Vendor Tiering Tool
- VRA Questionnaire
- Retesting Guidance
**Intent:** Assume commitment and provide technical clarity on execution steps.

## Phase 4: Human Confirmation (Trust Lock)
**Question Answered:** "Before I act, can a real expert sanity-check this?"
**Components:**
- HumanCheckCTA
- MonetizationCTA (Paid auditor intro)
**Intent:** Provide technical confirmation and decision confidence without a "sales demo" feel.
