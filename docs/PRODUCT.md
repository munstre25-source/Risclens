# Product Overview

## What It Does
- Provides SOC 2 readiness assessment with instant score and cost band.
- Educates through SOC 2 guides: cost, timeline, Type I vs II, checklist, cost breakdown, timing, startups, enterprise sales, SOC 2 vs ISO 27001, industry variants (SaaS, Fintech).
- Offers optional PDF/email of results after assessment.

## What It Does NOT Do
- No automated compliance enforcement or control monitoring.
- No authentication or user accounts.
- No paywalls; email optional for PDF delivery only.

## Primary CTA
- Single CTA across guides: “Get Your Readiness Score →” linking to `/soc-2-readiness-index`.

## Audience
- Early-stage to growth SaaS/fintech teams, enterprise sales stakeholders, and buyers comparing SOC 2 vs ISO 27001.
